# Glyph 180 BPM Metronome

A Glyph Toy for Nothing Phone (3) that pulses at exactly 180 BPM on the Glyph Matrix display. Designed for coaches, runners, and athletes who need a reliable cadence reference.

## What it does

- Plays a tick sound in sync with the flash, designed to fit the Nothing audio aesthetic
- Flashes a cross pattern on the Glyph Matrix at 180 BPM
- Starts automatically when selected in the Glyph Toy carousel

## Requirements

- Nothing Phone (3)

> **Note:** May be compatible with Nothing Phone (4a) Pro, however that device features a 15×15 px Glyph Matrix and the display pattern has not been tested or optimized for it.

## Installation

1. Download the latest APK from [Releases](../../releases)
2. Enable installation from unknown sources on your phone
3. Install the APK
4. Open the app — it will redirect you to the Glyph Toys manager
5. Add the metronome to your active toys

## Usage

- **Short press** the Glyph Button to cycle through toys
- The metronome starts automatically when selected

## Note

Due to Nothing OS restrictions, third-party Glyph Toys are limited to 60 seconds of runtime. After that, the metronome stops automatically. To restart, simply press the Glyph Button to cycle away and back to the metronome — it will start again automatically.
